Privacy Policy

This page explains what information we collect when you contact us or request an estimate, and how we use it. Last updated July 2026.

Who This Policy Covers

This policy applies to visitors of concretehouston.org, operated by Jerson's Concrete Houston Parking Lot Repair ("we," "us," "our"). It describes what information we collect through this website and how we handle it.

Information We Collect

When you submit our estimate request form, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email address, project city, project type, timeline, and project details. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect payment information through this website.

If you call the phone number listed on this site, that call is handled by our phone carrier, not by this website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your estimate request and schedule a site visit
  • To communicate with you about your project
  • To keep basic business records of inquiries and jobs

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising or marketing unrelated to your inquiry.

Third-Party Services We Use

Our estimate form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service, which delivers your submission to our email. Our contact page also embeds a Google Maps map. Our website is hosted through GitHub Pages and served through Cloudflare, which provides security and performance services and may set minimal technical cookies as part of that process. Each of these providers has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data that passes through its service.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for advertising or tracking. Cloudflare, our security and hosting provider, may set a small number of cookies strictly needed to keep the site secure and running properly. If you interact with the embedded Google Map, Google may set its own cookies under its own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We keep estimate requests and related communication for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your inquiry, complete a project, and maintain normal business records.

Your Rights

You can ask us what information we have about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, by calling 281-671-4809 or using our contact form.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking concrete construction and repair services. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
